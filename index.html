About Us

Legal and technical thinking in one project space.

The project is developed by a student team within JUS5671 and is grounded in the intersection of law, artificial intelligence and practical digital governance. It is designed to communicate credibility from the outset while remaining easy to maintain, understand and expand.

Project background

Data Protection Impact Assessments are increasingly relevant in environments where public authorities, private companies and legal professionals work with systems that may affect privacy rights and data protection obligations.

Our ambition is to develop a tool that supports a more structured approach to DPIA-related reasoning, while preserving the central role of legal analysis and contextual judgment. At this stage, the website functions as a professional shell that communicates the project’s direction and prepares for future technical integration.

What guides the project

Legal credibility The visual language and structure are intended to inspire confidence in legal and compliance contexts.
Practical clarity The solution should remain understandable for users without advanced technical backgrounds.
Future extensibility The current front-end is deliberately prepared for later integration of a functional DPIA module.
Cross-platform simplicity The project setup is intentionally minimal so it works cleanly across Windows and Mac environments.
